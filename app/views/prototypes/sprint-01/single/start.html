If your landlord has not resolved a problem with your privately rented house or flat after you have informed them in writing, use this service to find out what you can do next.

Types of problem could include maintenance or repairs to your home or issues with your tenancy agreement.

Next steps

You’ll get advice and guidance about what to do next and which organisations can help, including The Property Ombudsman, Shelter and Citizens’ Advice.

This service is only available to private rental tenants in England.

If you rent via a council or housing association you can get help from the Housing Ombudsman.
