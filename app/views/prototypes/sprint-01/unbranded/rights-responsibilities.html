Your rights and your landlord's responsibilities

Check your tenancy agreement to see if your problem is specifically covered. You can also see what your landlord's responsibilities are according to the law.

You said you had a problem with the following areas.

Temperature extremes

Your landlord is responsible for making sure that living conditions are not too hot or too cold. This includes working boilers and radiators. See more about your rights as a tenant in the Guide for tenants: Homes (Fitness for Human Habitation) Act 2018.

More information about landlord responsibilities

Find out more about what you and your landlord are responsible for on the Citizens advice website or the Shelter website.

Take pictures and keep a record

It may help to take pictures of the problem, especially if it changes. Keep copies of all letters, text messages or emails. Note the date and time of any phone calls.
