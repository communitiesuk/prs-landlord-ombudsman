Anti-social behaviour

Includes violence or intimidating language, harassment, discrimination and noise complaints.

  • Cutting off utilities
  • Violence or intimidating language from landlord
  • Harassment from landlord
  • Character discrimination
  • Rental discrimination
  • Landlord opening or withholding post
  • Entering property without permission
  • Poor or lack of communication
  • Unfounded or illicit demands
  • Noise complaints from other tenants
  • Removing or interfering with belongings and property
