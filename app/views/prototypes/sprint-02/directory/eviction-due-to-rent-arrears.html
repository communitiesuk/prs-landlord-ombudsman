Eviction due to rent arrears

If you’ve been threatened with eviction for not paying your rent as a private tenant, you need to speak to your landlord about your circumstances.

A notice of eviction from your landlord is the first legal step towards eviction.

Your landlord still has to apply to court.

If you can sort out your money or benefit problems, they may not take court action.

Anyone with a notice can get free legal advice and help at court.

You could also get legal aid help if you're on benefits or a low income.

You could:

  • get advice by phone or face to face
  • be referred for specialist debt or money advice

Visit the Ministry of Justice website to find free legal help in your area.
