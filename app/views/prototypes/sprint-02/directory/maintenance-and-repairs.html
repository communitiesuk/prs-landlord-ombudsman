If the home you rent needs repairs or maintenance

Includes gas, electricity, boiler or heating issues, damp or mould pest infestation, and plumbing or leaks.

Internal structural repairs

  • Boiler issues affecting hot water or heating
  • Gas, such as the smell of gas or a gas leak
  • Electricity or wiring
  • Water supply
  • Water coming in from outside
  • Leaks from plumbing or appliances
  • Toilet not working
  • Broken stairs, floorboards, or other shared common areas
  • Structural collapse or failing elements
  • Excessive cold or heat
  • Other issues, such as a dripping tap or internal door not closing

External structural repairs

  • External plumbing and leaks
  • Doors or windows not opening or closing
  • Damp and mould
  • Fire related
  • Faulty or missing appliances
  • Pest infestation
