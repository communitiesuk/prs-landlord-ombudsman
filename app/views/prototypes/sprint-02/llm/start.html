Getting help elsewhere

Your Local Authority

Your local authority can enforce your rights as a tenant and help you with problems with your property, such as repairs and maintenance.

You can find how to contact your local authority on the gov.uk website.

Citizens Advice

Citizens Advice can help you with problems with your property, such as repairs and maintenance.
