Create a landlord account

All private landlords in England with assured or regulated tenancies are required by law to:

  • join the Private Rented Sector Landlord Ombudsman Service, including those who use a managing agent
  • register themselves and their rental properties on the Private Rented Sector Database
{{ govukWarningText({ text: "Landlords could be subject to penalties if they market or let out a property without registering it and providing the required information.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

Use this service to:

  • register yourself and your rental properties on the Private Rented Sector Database
  • join the Private Rented Sector Landlord Ombudsman Service
  • add your contact details
  • add a tenant’s contact details
{% from "govuk/components/inset-text/macro.njk" import govukInsetText %} {{ govukInsetText({ text: "When you've completed registration, you can message your tenant to resolve an issue or dispute." }) }}

Before you start

You will need:

  • your tenant's email address and mobile phone number
  • your tenant's tenancy start date
