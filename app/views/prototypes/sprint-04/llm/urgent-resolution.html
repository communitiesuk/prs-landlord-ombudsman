How to get help with an urgent issue

For urgent safety concerns, like gas leaks, call the emergency services.

If you have other safety concerns about your rented home, you should first contact your their landlord or letting agent. If the issue is not resolved, contact your local council's housing department or other relevant bodies like Shelter England or Citizens Advice.
